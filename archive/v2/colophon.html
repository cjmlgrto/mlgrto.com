The Adventures Of CJ

  • Who am I anyway?

    I'm a hipster student who loves to frolick around on the web. I write posts and link stuff on this here blog, and I like to design stuff in my spare time. I also write code and play the piano whenever I have the chance.

    When I'm not writing for my site, elsewhere on the web, I shoot baskets at Dribbble, make short movies at YouTube, and I take amateur photographs at Flickr.

    I also love and serve the Lord God, as I am a Catholic, and I love Jesus as he is my savior.

    If you're wondering what I sit in front of whenever I blog, I have an amazing 27" iMac, an Apple Wireless Keyboard, an Apple Magic Mouse, an iPhone and an external 1TB hard drive, on which I use Time Machine to backup my important files with.

    I also have a secret stash of my favorite posts I've written. You can see that stash here.

  • Contacting me is a breeze.

    Commenting systems on blogs makes a great leeway for spammers and besides, I don't want random people from the internet selling me refurbished Android tablets and Levis.

    Although, for those of you who want to get ahold of me, you can email me here, or you can tweet at me on twitter with my username, @cjmlgrto.

  • You design stuff, right?

    Yeah, I do, but if you wanna see my portfolio, it's still in the works. In the meantime, you can look at my portfolio's progress over at Dribbble, or read my tweets as I rant about why that "div" can't position well.
