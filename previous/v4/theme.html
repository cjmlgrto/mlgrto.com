About The Backlog

The Backlog is CJ Melegrito's personal, online web-column. He writes here his thoughts, shares some interesting links and perhaps, occasionally post a photo or two. The phrase "Backlog" comes from a conjugation of the phrases "back-end" and "weblog".

About the Author

CJ Melegrito, if described in his own words, would be: a hipster student, front-end web developer, and a Gestalt-Ingenieur. You can read more about him in his colophon.

Elsewhere

CJ Melegrito frolicks a lot around the interweb. You can follow his tweets at Twitter, watch him shoot at Dribbble, or if you can, you could catch him shooting videos at either YouTube or Vimeo.
